About Sonic Horizon

Atmospheric soundscapes. Cinematic energy. Music beyond the horizon.

Who We Are

Sonic Horizon is a boundary-pushing alt-electronic rock band blending deep synth ambience, distorted guitars, and immersive storytelling. Formed by artists obsessed with cosmic sound and neon-lit atmospheres, the band creates experiences that feel like crossing into another world.

Our Sound

Inspired by sunsets, starfields, and futuristic cityscapes, our music fuses cinematic textures with pulse-driven rhythms. Every track carries the emotional weight of a journey—building, rising, glowing.

Journey So Far

With millions of global streams, multiple festival tours, and collaborations across genres, Sonic Horizon continues to expand its universe. From intimate synth sessions to explosive live shows, the band’s evolution remains endless.
